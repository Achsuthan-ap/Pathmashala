Pathmashalaa Offerings

Tailored Classes for All Ages

Apart from regular yogasana classes, Pathmashala provides tailored classes for different age groups and abilities.

Multi-level Classes

Classes are categorized into introductory, beginners, intermediate, and advanced levels, available in packages.

Complete Yoga Packages

Packages include Asana practice (stances), Pranayama (breathing), and Dhyana (meditation) to ensure holistic benefits.

Specialized Workshops

We organize theme-based workshops to help participants gain specialized knowledge in different aspects of Yoga.

Trained Yoga Instructors

Classes are led by trained Instructors, offering various styles including Hatha, Iyengar, Ashtanga, SKY, and Vinyasa.

Props and Aids for All Levels

Our yogashala is equipped with props and aids to help students of all levels maximize their Yoga practice.
